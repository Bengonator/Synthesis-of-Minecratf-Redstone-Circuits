
Example 1: know-it-all
	
	I wanted two torches behind each other, with only one solid block in between. The lower torch one should be lit and the upper one should be turned of.
	The solver quickly showed me that there is a way better smaller solution.
	
	! Due to many changes in the alogrithm, the result now differs !
	
	Settings:
	(define nCOLs 4)
	(define nROWs 5)
	(define xOFFSET 0)
	(define yOFFSET 0)
	(define zOFFSET 0)

	Goals:

	(add_lit_torch_goal 1 2)
